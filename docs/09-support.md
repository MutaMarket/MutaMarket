# Contact & Support

## How do I get help?

There are several ways to get help:

- Check our [Help Center](/help) for detailed guides
- Join our Discord community for support
- Contact us through the support system
- Check the FAQ section for common questions

## How do I report issues?

To report issues:

1. Use the "Report Issue" button on the relevant page
2. Provide as much detail as possible about the issue
3. Include screenshots if applicable
4. Specify what you were trying to do when the issue occurred

> **Important:** For urgent issues, please contact us through Discord for faster response.

## How do I suggest new features?

We welcome feature suggestions! You can:

- Post suggestions on our Discord server
- Use the feedback form on the website
- Contact us directly with your ideas

> **Note:** While we can't implement every suggestion, we carefully consider all feedback to improve MutaMarket.
