# Contract Management

## How do I open a contract in-game?

You have two options:

1. Right-click on the module and select "Open Contract In-Game" to access it directly.
2. Copy the contract link and paste it into the in-game notepad, as links cannot be pasted into the chat.

Both methods provide an easy way to view and manage your contract.

## How do I view a module in-game?

1. Right-click the module
2. Select "Copy Item Link"
3. Paste the link into the in-game notepad
4. Click the link to open and view the module
