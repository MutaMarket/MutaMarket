# Offers

## What are offers?

Offers are a way to negotiate module prices on MutaMarket. They provide:

- **Price Negotiation**: Make counter-offers on listed modules
- **Communication**: Direct messaging with sellers/buyers
- **Tracking**: View all your active offers and their status
- **Notifications**: Get notified when offers are accepted, rejected, or new messages arrive

> **Note:** Each offer includes the module details, estimated value, and a message thread for communication.

## How do I make an offer?

1. Find the module you're interested in on the [Modules page](/modules)
2. Click on the module to view its details
3. Click the "Make Offer" button
4. In the message field, include:
   - Your offer amount in ISK
   - Any additional information or reasoning for your offer
5. Submit your offer

> **Tips:**
>
> - Use the appraisal tool to help determine a fair offer price
> - Check similar modules on the market to gauge current prices
> - Be polite and professional in your messages
> - Clearly state your offer amount at the start of your message
> - Format your ISK amount clearly (e.g., "1.5b ISK" or "1,500,000,000 ISK")

## How do I manage my offers?

Visit the [Offers page](/offers) to manage all your offers. Here you can:

### View Offer Details

- See the module being offered
- View the estimated value
- Check who you're negotiating with
- Read the latest message in the conversation

### Take Action

- Click "Show offer" to view full details and message history
- Use "Leave offer" to withdraw from negotiations
- Respond to messages in the offer thread
- Communicate your acceptance or rejection through messages

> **Note:** New messages are marked with a red "New" badge to help you track unread communications.

## What is proper offer etiquette?

Following these guidelines helps ensure smooth negotiations:

- **Be Professional**: Keep communications polite and respectful
- **Be Clear**: Explain your reasoning for offer amounts
- **Be Responsive**: Respond to messages in a timely manner
- **Be Realistic**: Make offers based on market value and module stats
- **Be Patient**: Give sellers time to consider your offer

> **Important:**
>
> - If you're no longer interested, use the "Leave offer" button to properly end negotiations
> - Sellers can block users from contacting them if they receive inappropriate or harassing messages
> - Once blocked, you cannot make new offers to that seller

## How do I get notified about offers and messages?

MutaMarket provides several ways to stay informed about your offers:

### Discord Notifications

- Receive instant notifications in Discord when you get new offers
- Get notified about new messages in ongoing negotiations
- Link your Discord account in your profile settings to enable notifications

### Website Notifications

- See a red "New" badge on unread messages in the offers list
- View notification count in the navigation bar
- Get visual indicators for new offers on your modules

> **Note:** Discord notifications are the fastest way to stay updated on your offers. Make sure to link your Discord
> account in your profile settings.
